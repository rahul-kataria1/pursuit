FACILITY RULES & POLICIES

Pursuit OCR is strictly an 15+ facility. All guests under the age of 18 years must be accompanied by their Parent or Legal Guardian signing this waiver. ALL guests who are 18 years or older must present matching government issued ID. When listing the Parent/Legal Guardian on a minor's waiver, please enter their legal name exactly as it appears on their own waiver.

Admittance by booking only. Tickets can be purchased on the Booking & Pricing tab after you’ve filled out your waiver. ALL 18+ guests must fill out their own waiver before or at arrival.

ALL guests must be free of alcohol, cannabis & any recreational drugs to enter and must remain so throughout the entire duration of your visit. Any violation of this rule will result in the immediate termination of your entire party’s visit with no refund.

Athletic/running shoes that are fully closed-toe and lace up are mandatory, otherwise entry may be denied. Shoes not permitted are Slides, Crocs, boots, dress shoes, foam runners, heels/platform, etc. Barefoot or in socks participation is not allowed. Comfy clothing to run around in is recommended.

To make sure you have enough time to check in and get ready, we suggest arriving 10-15 minutes before your scheduled start time. If you booked a regular public session, this experience may allow date changes up to 48 hours in advance... There are no refunds permitted under any circumstances. For more information, you can consult Fever's T&Cs. Any decision to approve a credit is depends on various factors/availability.

Corporate Buyout or Private Events: Different rules may apply to corporate buyout and private events.

We have the right to refuse entry to anyone or any group if we feel it's necessary. This policy helps us make sure everyone – both our guests and our staff – can enjoy a welcoming and secure environment. If we deny entry, there are no refunds. For any other questions check out our FAQ, text or call and leave a voicemail at 647-467-8444 or email info@pursuitocr.com
