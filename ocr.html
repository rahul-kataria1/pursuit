Obstacle Course

A dramatic, energetic indoor training space featuring rugged, dark wood structures (climbing net, rings, slatted walls) under theatrical neon lighting. This is the main challenge area.

Go to guest policies
Physical Requirement:
Experience Type: Intermediate - Assumes basic fitness.
Accessibility: Limited - Requires climbing and jumping.
The Neon Gate
Available

The Neon Gate

The primary entrance and warm-up zone. Features soft flooring and light stretching equipment before you hit the course. Also acts as a waiting area.

Go to guest policies
Physical Requirement:
Experience Type: Easy - No obstacles involved.
Accessibility: Full - Ground level, wheelchair accessible.
The Cove Wall
Available

The Cove Wall

An iconic, smooth, curved ramp (like a quarter pipe) designed for high-speed runs and parkour-style climbs. Requires speed, timing, and upper body strength.

Go to guest policies
Physical Requirement:
Experience Type: Advanced - Expert-level technique needed.
Accessibility: Very Limited - High risk of falls.
Geometric Obstacle
Available

Geometric Obstacle

A series of soft foam blocks, cubes, and wedges used for climbing, tumbling, and practicing precision jumps. Ideal for younger visitors or warm-ups.

Go to guest policies
Physical Requirement:
Experience Type: Beginner - Fun and low impact.
Accessibility: Moderate - Requires balance and steps.
Tricycle Race Track
Available

Tricycle Race Track

A separate, dedicated asphalt track where visitors can race and drift large adult-sized tricycles. Great for team-building and low-impact fun.

Go to guest policies
Physical Requirement:
Experience Type: Easy - Cycling skill preferred.
Accessibility: Full - Accessible area, but requires riding capacity.
The Arena
Available

The Arena

A large, open area covered in soft sand, primarily used for team warm-ups, group fitness classes, and specialized sports like beach volleyball or training drills in challenging terrain.

Go to guest policies
Physical Requirement:
Experience Type: All Levels - Activities vary widely.
Accessibility: Limited - Sand surface is not wheelchair accessible.
